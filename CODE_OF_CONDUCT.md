# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone who contributes to or uses the Citizen Grievance NLP project.

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in communication.
- Demonstrate empathy and professionalism at all times.
- Share constructive feedback and collaborate in good faith.
- Be inclusive of all backgrounds, genders, nationalities, and perspectives.
- Honor project ownership and credit others for their contributions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes, but is not limited to:

- Harassment, bullying, or abusive language.
- Discrimination based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, nationality, disability, or any protected characteristic.
- Personal attacks, insults, or threats.
- Deliberate disruption of discussion or work.
- Inappropriate or offensive images, code, or content.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainers immediately.

### Contact
- Use the primary project communication channel agreed by the team.
- If the team is unable to resolve the issue, escalate to your instructor, mentor, or organization sponsor.

## Enforcement

Team maintainers may take action to address conduct violations, including:

- Asking individuals to revise or remove inappropriate content.
- Temporarily blocking project access.
- Removing contributions that violate this code.

The goal is to maintain a positive environment where the entire group of three can collaborate effectively.

## Attribution

This code of conduct is adapted from common open source community standards to fit our group project.
